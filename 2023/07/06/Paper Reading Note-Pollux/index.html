Paper Reading Note of OSDI2021 Best Paper - Pollux

Created2023-07-06|Updated2023-07-08|Paper
|Post View:

Abstract

Pollux improves scheduling performance in deep learning (DL) clusters by adaptively co-optimizing inter-dependent factors both at the per-job level and at the cluster-wide level. Most existing schedulers expect users to specify the number of resources for each job, often leading to inefficient resource use. Some recent schedulers choose job resources for users, but do so without awareness of how DL training can be re-optimized to better utilize the provided resources.

可以说，常规的 schedule 考虑的是如何为任务调度资源，任务有需求，就接受这个需求，然后看系统资源怎么分配调度比较好。但是 Pollux 相当于提出来一个质疑，这些需求真的合理吗，每个 node 要那么多资源，有没有可能是任务的执行没有优化好，如果不仅优化全局的资源调度，还优化任务的参数配置，能不能更好的提升整个系统的效率？

Pollux simultaneously considers both aspects. By monitoring the status of each job during training, Pollux models how their goodput (a metric we introduce to combine system throughput with statistical efficiency) would change by adding or removing resources. Pollux dynamically (re-) assigns resources to improve cluster-wide goodput, while respecting fairness and continually optimizing each DL job to better utilize those resources.

那一个任务执行的到底好不好，得想个办法量化一下，这样就有了 target，然后可以学习参数去优化，任务训练时的参数改变了，就要影响到任务需要的资源，于是乎可以得到 adding or removing resources 对 target 的影响。所以这里定义了一个 goodput，它既考虑了系统的吞吐 throughput，还考虑了系统的统计效率 statistical efficiency。

这里思考一下，无论是什么手段，优化系统调度的目的是什么？应该是在有限的资源下，尽可能地让分布式 ML 变得又快又准，即最大程度地减少训练时间和资源消耗，提高整体的训练速度和性能 (模型质量)。那么这里的 goodput 就有这个意味，吞吐量和数据处理速度相关，statistical efficiency 和任务处理的质量有关，statistical efficiency 可以简单理解成在一个样本上迭代后模型精度能够进步多少。

In experiments with real DL jobs and with trace-driven simulations, Pollux reduces average job completion times by 37–50% relative to state-of-the-art DL schedulers, even when they are provided with ideal resource and training configurations for every job. Pollux promotes fairness among DL jobs competing for resources, based on a more meaningful measure of useful job progress, and reveals a new opportunity for reducing DL cost in cloud environments. Pollux is implemented and publicly available as part of an open-source project at https://github.com/petuum/adaptdl.

Pollux 的 idea 新颖，实验结果也非常好，Best Paper 是有原因的。而且这里有意思的是还考虑了公平性问题，这里暂时有个疑问，公平性如何定义？为什么要考虑公平性问题？对整个分布式训练的总效果有价值吗? 为了公平而公平会不会导致总的模型效果反而变差呢？后面看看作者是怎么说的。

Introduction

DL 任务对资源要求高且耗时长，通常需要（如 GPU 或 TPU）进行分布式执行，以在合理的时间内完成。通常会为深度学习配置专用集群，其中包括一个调度器来协调多个竞争的深度学习任务之间的资源共享。

老办法是手动分配，但是显然容易出问题，于是有了动态分配策略。但是主流的动态分配的调度器都直接忽略了任务内部的statistical efficiency，只考虑throughput。

但是想计算任务的 statistical efficiency 不是一件易事，在配置深度学习任务的过程中，resources、batch-size 和 lr 的选择并不容易，需要对集群硬件性能和深度学习模型架构都有专业知识。而且由于它们的最佳值之间存在相互依赖关系，在设置时要同时考虑。并且由于共享集群的动态性质，它们的最佳值可能随时间变化，所以并不是一个给定条件的最优化问题，比较复杂。

同时 system throughput 和 statistical efficiency 还存在制约：

How can a cluster scheduler help to automatically configure user-submitted DL jobs? Fundamentally, a properly configured DL job strikes a balance between two often opposing desires: (1) system throughput, the number of training examples processed per wall-clock time, and (2) statistical efficiency, the amount of progress made per training example processed.

这里之所以说 wall-clock time 应该是强调真实时间而不是只考虑数据处理时间忽略数据加载、异常中断延迟等时间。而之所以说 system throughput 和 statistical efficiency 之间是 opposing 的，直观上看就是 batch-size 增大后吞吐增加但是相当于用更多的样本完成一轮训练，这样分配到每个样本上，per training example 对于模型的效果提升的贡献 the amount of progress 而言就比较小了。因此，增大 batch size 反而会降低 statistical efficiency。

在 batch-size，resource scalability，stage of training 之间存在 trade-offs，这还是已经将 lr 调优了的情况

上图的结果是：

  • 硬件增加是能够提供更高的 throughput 的，这很符合直觉，算力加大吞吐增加
  • system throughput 是可以通过增加 batch size 来提高的，因为更大的 batch size 能够增加计算设备的资源使用率，也合理，batch-size 大了就能把显存跑得更满
  • 但是 batch size 增加往往会导致 statistical efficiency 降低（参考 figure 1. a），这个也符合直觉，刚才上一段的分析中也提到了。
  • 因此对于固定数量的设备，最优的 batch size 配置往往是不同的（参考 figure 1. b），也就是说训练的不同阶段最佳 batch-size 是不同的。

基于上面的认识，作者提出了 Pollux，一种混合资源调度器，可以自适应地分配资源并调整共享集群中所有 DL 作业的批量大小和学习率。简单来说就是用一个新的指标 goodput 来指导对集群资源的调度优化，其中 goodput 综合考虑了 throughput 和 efficiency，

Background

开头作者简单提了下 distributed ML/DL 的基础知识

System Throughput

影响 system throughput 的因素：

When a DL job is distributed across several nodes, its system throughput is determined by several factors, including (1) the allocation and placement of resources (e.g. GPU s) assigned to the job, (2) the method of distributed execution and synchronization, and (3) the batch size.

怎么被这些因素影响的呢？

以 Synchronous data-parallelism 运行场景为例，运行时间由两部分决定，TgradT_{grad}Tgrad​ 和 TsyncT_{sync}Tsync​，这两部分时间从字面意思已经可以理解，不再分析。

而 TgradT_{grad}Tgrad​ 和 TsyncT_{sync}Tsync​ 受一些因素的影响：

TsyncT_{sync}Tsync​ is influenced by the size of the gradients, performance of the network, and is typically shorter when the GPU s are co-located within the same physical node or rack.

TgradT_{grad}Tgrad​ decreases due to a smaller per-GPU batch size. On the other hand, TsyncT_{sync}Tsync​, which is typically independent of the batch size, remains unchanged. By Amdahl ’s Law, no matter how many GPU s are used, the run-time of each training iteration is lower bounded by TsyncT_{sync}Tsync​. To overcome this scalability limitation, a common strategy is to increase the batch size. Doing so causes the local gradient estimates to be computed over more training examples and thereby increasing the ratio of TgradT_{grad}Tgrad​ to TsyncT_{sync}Tsync​. As a result, using a larger batch size enables higher system throughput when scaling to more GPU s in the synchronous data-parallel setting.

在训练过程中 TsyncT_{sync}Tsync​ 是存在 lower bound 的，减少 batch-size 也不能降低这部分时间的消耗，所以一般采用增大 batch-size 的手段，看起来会算的更多更快，而且能够减少通信次数进而降低同步时间的影响。因此，使用更大的批量大小在 Synchronous data-parallelism 设置中能够实现更高的系统吞吐量。

Statistical Efficiency

给出了定义：The statistical efficiency of DL training can be defined as the amount of training progress made per unit of training data processed, influenced by parameters such as batch size or learning rate.

涉及到后续 modeling 的一句话是：The ability to predict statistical efficiency is key to improving said statistical efficiency, because we can use the predictions to better adapt the batch sizes and learning rates.

Gradient noise scale

可以理解成 gradient 的抗干扰能力，这个值越大 batch size 与 learning rate 就算设置的较大也不容易降低 statistical efficiency；另外，这个值不同的模型之间变化很大，而且在一个模型训练过程中一般也会越来越大。

由于之前没有了解过这个概念，这里分析一下原文：

The gradient noise scale mathematically captures an intuitive explanation of how the batch size affects statistical efficiency. When the stochastic gradient has low noise, adding more training examples to each mini-batch does not significantly improve each gradient estimate, which lowers statistical efficiency. When the stochastic gradient has high noise, adding more training examples to each mini-batch reduces the noise of each gradient estimate, which maintains high statistical efficiency. Near convergence, the stochastic gradients have relatively lower signal than noise, and so larger batch sizes can be more useful later in training.

这段话可以理解为，当梯度估计中的噪声较低时，增加批量大小对统计效率的提升有限；而当梯度估计中的噪声较高时，增加批量大小可以降低梯度估计的噪声，从而保持较高的统计效率。特别是在接近收敛时，使用较大的批量大小可能更有益。

Learning rate scaling

训练过程当中，如果 batch size 增大，那么 learning rate 也应该做相应的调整，否则会降低模型训练最终的精度。learning rate scaling 的策略有许多，比如 linear scaling rule, square-root scaling rule。具体内容就不展开了。

Existing DL Schedulers

作者将当前现有的调度器分为两种：

  • First, non-scale-adaptive schedulers are agnostic to the performance scalability of DL jobs with respect to the amount of allocated resources

  • Second, scale-adaptive schedulers automatically decide the amount of resources allocated to each job based on how well they can be utilized to speed up the job.

第一种是非自适应的调度器，这种调度器不会根据任务的需求和性能特点来调整资源的分配。第二种是自适应调度器，自适应调度器会根据任务的需求和性能特点智能地调整资源的分配。

Crucially, existing schedulers are agnostic to the statistical efficiency of DL training and the inter-dependence of resource decisions and training parameters. Pollux explicitly co-adapts these inter-dependent values to improve goodput for DL jobs.

现有调度器忽视了 DL 训练的统计效率以及资源决策与训练参数之间的相互关系，而 Pollux 调度器则通过协同调整这些值来提高 DL 任务的效率和性能。

不过作者在论文下面也备注了 Pollux 还可以改善的地方，Pollux’s current throughput model does not consider accelerator heterogeneity. We believe that extending with Gavel’s metric would allow Pollux to co-adapt for goodput in heterogeneous DL clusters.

"Pre-conditioned Gradient Noise Scale (PGNS)"与普通的梯度噪声尺度（GNS）之间的区别

假设我们有一个深度神经网络模型，在进行训练时使用了一种优化算法（如随机梯度下降），并计算了每个训练批次的梯度。

首先，我们可以计算每个训练批次的梯度的标准差，作为GNS的度量。这可以帮助我们了解每个批次中梯度的波动情况，即普通的梯度噪声尺度。然而，使用PGNS时，我们在计算梯度的标准差之前对梯度进行了预处理。预处理可以是一种数学转换或变换操作，旨在减少梯度噪声的影响并提高其可解释性。

例如，一种常见的预处理方法是通过对梯度进行归一化或标准化来使其具有相似的尺度。这样可以确保不同参数或层的梯度具有相对一致的范围，减少梯度差异的影响。通过预处理梯度后，我们可以计算PGNS，即经过预处理的梯度噪声尺度。PGNS提供了一个更准确和可控的度量，可以更好地反映梯度噪声对模型训练的影响。

总结起来，与普通的梯度噪声尺度相比，PGNS通过在计算之前对梯度进行预处理，提供了更可解释和可控的梯度噪声度量。这有助于研究人员更好地理解和控制梯度噪声的影响，并采取相应的策略来优化模型的训练过程。

Pollux Design and Architecture

原文中一句话总结了核心内容：PolluxAgent and PolluxSched co-adapt to each other. While PolluxAgent adapts each training job to make efficient use of its allocated resources, PolluxSched dynamically re-allocates each job’s resources, taking into account the PolluxAgent’s ability to tune its job.

PolluxAgent: Job-level Optimization

Agent 做两件事情：

  1. continually measures the job’s gradient noise scale and system throughput, and it reports them to PolluxSched at a fixed interval.
  2. uses this information（GNS、throughput） to determine the most efficient batch size for its job given its current resource allocations, and adapts its job’s learning rate to this batch size using the appropriate plug-in LR scaling rule (e.g. AdaScale for SGD or square-root scaling for Adam).

注意第二个事情中的顺序，是先确定最优的 batch-size，然后再根据 LR scaling rule 调整 lr

Interference avoidance

Interference avoidance is implemented as a constraint in Pollux’s search algorithm, by ensuring at most one distributed job is allocated to each node.

JCT

JCT是Job Completion Time（作业完成时间）的缩写。在任务调度和作业流理论中，JCT表示作业或任务完成的时间点。它是指从作业或任务开始执行到完成的时间间隔。

在提到"tail JCT"时，"tail"是指JCT的尾部部分，也就是指作业或任务完成时间中的最后一部分。这种情况下，“tail JCT"通常用于描述作业或任务执行过程中的延迟或慢速部分。通过关注"tail JCT”，可以帮助识别和解决导致任务执行时间延长的问题，从而优化系统的性能和效率。

Conclusion

Pollux is a DL cluster scheduler that co-adaptively allocates resources, while at the same time tuning each training job to best utilize those resources. We present a formulation of goodput that combines system throughput and statistical efficiency for distributed DL training. Based on the principle of goodput maximization, Pollux automatically and jointly tunes the resource allocations, batch sizes, and learning rates for DL jobs, which can be particularly difficult for users to configure manually. Pollux outperforms and is more fair than recent DL schedulers, even if users can configure their jobs well, and provides even bigger benefits with more realistic user knowledge.
